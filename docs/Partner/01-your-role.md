# 01 - Your Role

Navigation: `README.md` | Next: `02-first-hour.md`

You are the owner of direction, priorities, and final decisions.

Agent1 is the owner of continuity, execution stamina, and deterministic follow-through.

## What You Decide

- What matters now.
- What "done" means.
- When risk is acceptable.
- When to merge, close, pause, or escalate.

## What Agent1 Carries

- Trigger handling (`@mentions`, assignments, review requests, CI changes).
- Job progression across states until terminal outcomes.
- Durable context from events, attempts, and transitions.
- Policy-constrained execution and reporting.

## The Core Partnership Rule

Agent1 does not replace your judgment. It compounds it.

You focus on intent and tradeoffs. Agent1 handles operational drag: rechecks, retries, correlation, and memory.

## Anti-Pattern To Avoid

Do not treat Agent1 like a one-shot command line helper.

When you do that, you lose its strongest property: long-horizon continuity across issue and PR lifecycles.
